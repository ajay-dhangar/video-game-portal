Shooter Games

Shooter games are games where the player uses firearms to shoot other players or targets to win. These games range from battle royale to fast-paced, tactical action. Here are some examples of shooter games!

Apex Legends

Apex Legends is a Hero Shooter Battle Royale from Respawn Entertainment. Master an ever-growing roster of legendary characters with powerful abilities, and experience strategic squad play and innovative gameplay.

Titanfall 2

Titanfall 2 delivers fast-paced, hyperkinetic vertical warfare that gives shooter fans the freedom to fight as elite assault Pilots and heavily armored Titans.

Destiny 2

Destiny 2 is a game where you harness the power of light to push back the forces of darkness, with a choice of armor, weapons, and special abilities.

DOOM

Hell's armies have invaded Earth. Become the Slayer in an epic single-player campaign to conquer demons across dimensions and stop the final destruction of humanity.

Battlefield V

Enter mankind's greatest conflict with the complete arsenal of weapons, vehicles, and gadgets in this realistic shooter.

Deep Rock Galactic

Deep Rock Galactic is a 1-4 player co-op FPS featuring badass space Dwarves, 100% destructible environments, procedurally generated caves, and endless hordes of alien monsters.
